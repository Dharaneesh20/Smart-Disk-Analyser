System Requirements

Windows Requirements

OS: Windows 10 or Windows 11 (x64)
Java: Java 17 or higher (included or auto-detected)
RAM: 4 GB minimum, 8 GB recommended
Disk: 500 MB free space
Permissions: Administrator rights (for partition operations)

Linux Requirements

OS: Debian 11+, Ubuntu 20.04+, Linux Mint 20+
Java: OpenJDK 17 or higher (auto-installed with package)
RAM: 4 GB minimum, 8 GB recommended
Disk: 500 MB free space
Permissions: Root/sudo access (for partition operations)
Desktop: GNOME, KDE, XFCE, or other DE
